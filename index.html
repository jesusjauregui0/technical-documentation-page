How to make French Toast

Grab a Bowl
  1. Favorite Bowl
  2. 2 Eggs and Cinnamon and butter
  3. Loaf of Bread
  4. Whisk
  5. Frying Pan
Grab your favorite bowl, April 30, 2020

The bowl will be used to mix in all the ingredients that will be needed for this recipe.

Make sure you get a bow that is easy to wash so once the cooking is done clean up will be just as easy!

Grab Eggs and Cinnamon
Make sure you have your eggs and cinnamon ready!, April 30, 2020

The eggs and the cinnamon are the ingredients that make up the taste of the french toast.

Make sure to use the freshest eggs and only a spoon or 2 of cinnamon.

Mix Eggs and Cinnamon
Use the whisk to mix both the eggs and cinnamon, then add pour the mix on a loaf of bread, Dec 7, 2017

Make sure to mix them properly using the whisk

once you're done mixing go ahead and impregnate a slice of bread to get it ready for cooking.

Heat up Frying Pan
Make sure to heat up the frying pan and add butter so the toast will not stick onto the pan, April 30, 2020

the butter is essential for making a perfect piece of toast make sureto add enough but not too much.

try using a non stick pan and wait until the butter is fully melted and spread all over the pan to reduce sticking.

Cook Mix in Pan
Once the mix has impregnated the loaf of bread you can lay it on the frying pan for 2 minutes each side!, April 30, 2020

make sure to not overcook it but that is how to make french toast!

Enjoy!
